> **Draft.** This is a working draft of the MemoryBinder Privacy Policy. The
> final version will be reviewed by independent legal counsel before
> launch. Treat the contents below as our intent, not a binding contract.

## 1. About this policy

MemoryBinder Pty Ltd ("MemoryBinder", "we", "us", "our") is the company
behind the MemoryBinder app and the MemoryBinder Registry. We build legal
property infrastructure for personal data, memories, and consumer AI
agents.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and protect
personal information across the MemoryBinder ecosystem. It is written to
be readable. Where it falls short of what the law requires, we apply the
higher standard. This Policy should be read in conjunction with our
[Terms of Use](/legal/terms).

Questions can be directed to our Privacy Officer at
**privacy@memorybinder.io**.

## 2. What we collect

We collect only what we need to deliver MemoryBinder and to keep the
legal record honest.

- **Identity information** — your name, contact details, and any
  government-issued identification you choose to provide for verified
  authentication.
- **Account credentials** — your username, password hash, and basic
  profile details.
- **Content you upload** — photos, audio, video, stories, and any other
  memory you place in your wallets. This content is your property. We
  do not own it. We register the legal record around it.
- **Provenance metadata** — the legal record we generate when you
  authenticate, transfer, license or share an asset. This is the public
  registry layer.
- **Usage data** — how you interact with the app, in aggregate, to make
  it better.

## 3. How we use what we collect

- To operate MemoryBinder and the Registry.
- To authenticate, verify, transfer and license assets you place under
  our care.
- To meet our legal obligations.
- To improve the product, with anonymised analytics. **Never** by
  feeding your content into model training without your explicit,
  granular, revocable consent.

## 4. The Data Trust

The MemoryBinder Registry is held in trust by an independent Data Trust,
separate from the MemoryBinder commercial entity. The Trust's charter is
public. The Trust has the authority to refuse uses of the Registry that
contravene the Trust's charter — including by us.

## 5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not feed your memories into AI
training. The only situations in which information leaves your control
are:

- You choose to share, gift, license, or sell an asset.
- We are legally compelled by a court of competent jurisdiction.
- A vendor we use to deliver the service (payment processor, cloud
  host) processes data on our behalf, under a written agreement that
  binds them to this Policy.

## 6. Your rights

You can access, correct, export, and delete personal information at any
time. The provenance record is append-only — but you can revoke access,
withdraw assets from circulation, and remove your account.

## 7. Security

We follow industry standard practice. We encrypt data in transit and at
rest. We log access to the Registry. We will tell you, promptly, if a
breach affects you.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We will tell you, in advance, before we change this Policy in any
material way. The current version always lives at
**memorybinder.io/legal/privacy**.
